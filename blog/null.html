things I learned from experience

jump rope

  • relieves stress and anxiety
  • improves overall mood
  • increases energy level

zone 2 running

  • improved sleep
  • increased confidence
  • relieves stress

    • problems that I went through:

    • runner's low/post-exercise depression
    • restless legs syndrome happens I stop running for a few days
    • feeling the need to nap after running

    reasons why I quit running:

    • scared of feeling lost again
    • not wanting to run to "escape" my thoughts

meditation

  • controls anxiety
  • reduces stress
  • provides a sense of calm, peace, and balance
  • increases concentration
  • increases empathy and compassion

sleeping on the floor

  • feeling more refreshed and well rested
  • improved posture
  • never felt groggy after waking up

cold shower

  • feel alert and refreshed
  • reduces inflammation
  • relieves pain

journaling

  • a way to process emotions
  • a good way to release when feeling overwhelmed
  • reduces anxiety and stress
